A Synopsis of IT Ethics

Information technology (IT) ethics is about using technology responsibly to protect people, businesses, and society as a whole. It includes things like security, privacy, openness, and how new technologies will affect different stakeholders.

Understanding and upholding IT ethics is critical to creating a digital environment that values fairness, respect, and trust in the face of the continued importance of technology in our daily lives.
