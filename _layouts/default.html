Introduction to IT Ethics

Information Technology (IT) ethics involves the responsible use of technology to ensure the well-being of individuals, organizations, and society at large. It encompasses considerations such as privacy, security, transparency, and the impact of technological innovations on various stakeholders.

As technology continues to play a crucial role in our daily lives, understanding and practicing IT ethics is essential to foster a digital environment that promotes trust, fairness, and respect for all.
